**Lime Help**

**Commands**

_/play_ _song name_ _- plays a song, searches based of the title entered_

_e.g. /play Stonks_

_/next_ _- plays the next song in the playlist, disconnects if there is none_

_e.g. /next_

_/stop_ _- stops the music_

_e.g. /stop_
